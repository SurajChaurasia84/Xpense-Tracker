Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2026

Welcome to Xpense Tracker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Xpense Tracker collects certain information to provide and improve our financial tracking services:

Personal Information

  • Authentication Data: When you sign in using Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture URL from Google. This is used solely to create and manage your user account.

Financial Data

  • Transactions: Information about your income and expenses, including titles, amounts, categories, and timestamps.
  • Budgets: Data regarding the spending limits and financial goals you set within the app.

Device & Security Data

  • App Preferences: Local settings such as whether the App Lock (Biometric) is enabled are stored on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • Biometric Data: Our app uses your device's native biometric sensors (Fingerprint/Face ID) for secure access. We do not collect, store, or have access to your actual biometric templates. All biometric authentication is handled by the operating system of your device.
  • Version Information: We may access your app version and build number to ensure compatibility and provide updates.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the application's core features (Tracking & Budgeting).
  • To personalize your experience (e.g., displaying your profile name/picture).
  • To secure your financial records through user authentication and optional biometric locks.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries via email.
  • To analyze app performance and fix bugs for a smoother experience.

3. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

We utilize industry-standard services to handle your data securely:

  • Google Firebase: Your authentication details (Google Login) and financial records (Transactions/Budgets) are securely stored in Google's Firebase Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication systems.
  • Local Storage: Minor settings (like App Lock status) are stored locally on your device and are not shared with any external servers.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to outside parties for marketing purposes.

4. Security of Your Data

The security of your financial information is our top priority. We implement several layers of protection:

  • Cloud Security: We use Firebase Security Rules to ensure that only you can access your own data.
  • App Lock: You can enable a biometric lock within the app settings for an extra layer of local security.
  • Encryption: Data transmitted between the app and Firebase servers is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.

5. Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data within Xpense Tracker:

  • Access & Edit: You can view and modify your transactions and budgets at any time.
  • Sign Out: You can sign out of the app to clear your current session.
  • Data Deletion: If you wish to delete your account or specific data, please contact us at the email provided below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
